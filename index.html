Accessible To Everyone

Geo Nodes Guide is an educational Blender addon focused entirely on helping artists and technical users understand Geometry Nodes. No magic buttons. No shortcuts. Just clear explanations, examples, and structure.

The goal is simple: make Geometry Nodes less intimidating and more predictable, whether you’re learning the basics or refining workflows.
